About Us

Bangladesh School Bahrain (BSB) has been a cornerstone of the Bangladeshi community in Bahrain since its establishment in 1995. Our mission is to provide high-quality education rooted in Bangladeshi values and culture, while preparing students for global challenges.

We believe in fostering a holistic learning environment that encourages academic excellence, moral development, and community engagement. Our vision is to be a leading educational institution that empowers students to become responsible citizens and future leaders, contributing positively to both Bahrain and Bangladesh.

Our Academics

At Bangladesh School Bahrain, we are dedicated to providing a comprehensive and challenging educational experience. We offer two distinct curricula to cater to diverse learning needs and future aspirations, ensuring that every student receives a world-class education.

National Curriculum of Bangladesh

Our primary curriculum follows the guidelines of the National Curriculum and Textbook Board (NCTB) of Bangladesh. This curriculum emphasizes Bangladeshi culture, history, and values, preparing students for the SSC and HSC examinations. It ensures a strong foundation in Bengali language, literature, and national identity while meeting international academic standards.

Explore BD Curriculum

Cambridge International Curriculum

For students seeking an internationally recognized qualification, we offer the Cambridge International Curriculum. This program is designed to develop critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and independent learning. Students are prepared for Cambridge IGCSE and A-Level examinations, providing pathways to leading universities worldwide.

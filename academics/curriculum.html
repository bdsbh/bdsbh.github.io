Our Comprehensive Curriculum

At Bangladesh School Bahrain, we are committed to providing a diverse and comprehensive educational experience. We proudly offer two distinct and highly respected curricula to cater to the varied needs and aspirations of our students, preparing them for success in a globalized world.

Bangladeshi NCTB English Version

Our core offering, the National Curriculum and Textbook Board (NCTB) English Version, is designed to provide a rich learning experience rooted in Bangladeshi heritage while fostering global competencies. This curriculum is approved by the Ministry of Education, Bangladesh, and is tailored to meet the academic requirements for students planning to pursue higher education in Bangladesh or other countries that recognize the NCTB framework.

Key features of the NCTB English Version curriculum include:

  • Strong foundation in Bengali language and literature, history, and culture.
  • Comprehensive coverage of core subjects: Mathematics, Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), English, Social Science, and Information & Communication Technology (ICT).
  • Emphasis on moral and character development, instilling values of respect, integrity, and community responsibility.
  • Prepares students for the SSC (Secondary School Certificate) and HSC (Higher Secondary Certificate) examinations conducted by the education boards in Bangladesh.

Our dedicated teachers, well-versed in the NCTB methodology, employ engaging teaching strategies to ensure students grasp concepts thoroughly and develop a strong sense of national identity alongside international understanding.

Cambridge IGCSE

For students aspiring to an internationally recognized qualification, Bangladesh School Bahrain offers the Cambridge International General Certificate of Secondary Education (IGCSE). This prestigious curriculum, developed by Cambridge Assessment International Education, is followed by schools in over 160 countries worldwide, providing a globally benchmarked education.

Key benefits of the Cambridge IGCSE include:

  • Internationally recognized and respected by universities and employers worldwide.
  • Develops subject content, critical thinking, problem-solving, and investigative skills.
  • Broad and balanced curriculum allowing for a wide choice of subjects.
  • Promotes a deeper understanding of subjects rather than rote learning.
  • Provides a strong foundation for advanced academic study.

Students can choose from a variety of subjects, including:

  • English (First Language, Second Language)
  • Mathematics (Core, Extended, Additional)
  • Sciences (Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Coordinated Science)
  • Humanities (History, Geography, Business Studies, Economics, Global Perspectives)
  • Arts and Technology (Art & Design, ICT)

Our dedicated IGCSE teachers provide expert guidance, ensuring students are well-prepared for the rigorous external examinations and equipped with skills for lifelong learning.
